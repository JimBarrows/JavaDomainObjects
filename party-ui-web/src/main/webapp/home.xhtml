Hello and welcome to Little Black Organizer!

Little Black Organizer is the only organizer that looks at your tasks lists, calendars, contacts and all your other information as an integrated whole. Sometimes tasks are mini-projects. They involve people, and meetings and length conversations, all of which you have to remember day to day. Now, you can store it all in one place, accessible from anywhere and it can work the way you want it to work. Prefer to work on paper, and just store information online? We can do it. Work exclusively online? From your black berry, iphone, smart phone or PDA? We can do it.

Work the way you want to, and see your entire life in one place!
